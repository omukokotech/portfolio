About Me

If our ideas were all that mattered, then we would overcome most of life's challenges. For nearly every problem, the human mind has imagined a solution, and there is rarely an opportunity that has escaped the human eye on this planet and beyond. This is true for any part of the world as it is for Africa, where there are plenty of quality ideas. Africans from different generations have imagined the future they want and expressed it through art, songs, poems, and books. They have understood quite well what they need to do and how to do it to realize their dreams. Yet one critical piece is missing in this puzzle - capital. Think of capital as wheels on which ideas move or the engine that powers our dreams. Without capital, our ideas never see the light of day; our best efforts are merely efforts at best, and our dreams chase us away instead of us chasing them. This realization has sparked a deep interest in finance in me. How can we bridge the gap between ideas and capital? That is the question I want to solve, especially for young people who want their voices, talents, and ideas to contribute to shaping the future. When that becomes true, someday, we will harness much more of the enormous potential of the human mind. While lack of capital has been the difference between what is and what can be, it has unfortunately also been the difference between leading a life of dignity and one without. With basic needs on the line, bridging capital and ideas remains crucial. All who advance noble causes in all spaces appreciate this. Whether fighting climate change, ending world poverty, strengthening democratic leadership, or promoting equality, it all goes back to this critical question. It is now clear why I am easily drawn to finance, but there are more reasons. Firstly, there is much room for greater efficiency in allocating existing capital, and I am keen to use my knowledge of finance to promote this efficiency. I am also fascinated by the fact that numbers can tell a story, and I want to tell a good story that fosters trust and collaboration as we sail this ship called humanity across the ocean of life. I also want to challenge myself and see what lies on the other side of human potential. I am keen to learn new things, grow every day, and work with others to capture the power of synergy and the blessings of diversity. Let us talk about finance; let's talk about anything. I am open to opportunities that allow me to challenge myself, learn, collaborate, grow, and make a difference - a positive one!
